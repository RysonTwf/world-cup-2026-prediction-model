About the Model

Elo + Dixon-Coles bivariate Poisson · Walk-forward out-of-sample validation · 811 matches evaluated

A transparent, reproducible statistical model for international football. Team strength is measured via Elo ratings calibrated on 961 internationals (Oct 2023–Jun 2026). Match probabilities use a Dixon-Coles bivariate Poisson correction that fixes vanilla Poisson's tendency to undercount 0-0 and 1-1 draws. All predictions are deterministic from frozen ratings — no neural networks, no proprietary data.

Methodology

1
Elo Ratings 70% walk-forward fitted + 30% long-run prior. K-factor by competition. 18-month recency half-life.
2
Expected Goals λ = 1.35 + ΔElo / 400, clamped [0.3, 3.5]. Home bonus = +150 Elo on the home team's attack rate only.
3
Dixon-Coles Match Model Bivariate Poisson over 0–8 goals per side. DC ρ = −0.13 corrects under-count of low-scoring draws.
4
Frozen at Kickoff No mid-tournament re-fitting. Stakes adjustments are separate labeled parameters, not folded into Elo.
